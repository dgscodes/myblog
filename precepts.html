Mr Brown's Precepts

Month Precept Interpretation
September 'When given the choice between being right or being kind, choose kind'

- Dr. Wayne W. Dyer		 Letting go is sometimes necessary, even if you know you're right, and it's fair to do to another as they've done to you - sometimes being kind is the better option, even if you're not doing what's considered fair or right.
October 'Your deeds are your monuments

-inscription on an Egyptian tomb		 What you do, your acts of kindness, your contribution to society, is your legacy which is on display as is a statue - representing the kind of person you are, a legacy which lasts for decades. You will die without any posessions, but be remembered for your deeds.
December Audentes fortuna iuvat (fortune favours the bold)

- Virgil		 You have to be bold in order to be 'lucky'. To be successful means to take risks, to be willing to fail.
June 'Just follow the day and reach for the sun'

- The Polyphonic Spree		 Follow through on your dreams, even through the toughest of days, in order to make your dream a reality. Just go with the flow, and make the best of adversities because those challenges make the dream even more rewarding once accomplished.
